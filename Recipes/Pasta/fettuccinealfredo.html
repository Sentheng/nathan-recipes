Fettuccine Alfredo

A classic (and healthy) take on the italian staple dish with a rich cheese sauce and fettuccine type of pasta.

Ingredients

  • 14 oz pasta Fettuccine
  • 2.5 cups cauliflowers florets
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 tablespoon of unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup grated parmigiano
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 2 teaspoon of finely chopped thyme
  • Salt + Pepper

Instructions

  1. Remove and chop the heads of cauliflowers (2 cups)
  2. Place caulifowers in a medium sauce pan, add milk and unsalted butter, boil over medium heat
  3. Once boiling, lower heat to medium-low for 12-15 minutes
  4. Remove from heat
  5. Add cauliflowers, garlic, thyme, grated parmigiano, and season with salt and pepper in blender and blend until smooth
  6. Place fettuccine into a pot of boiling water with seasoned geneously with salt
  7. Drain pasta (Save some pasta water for later)
  8. Place pasta on a bowl and cover with the sauce, mixed/tossed until evenly mixed
  9. Serve on a plate
