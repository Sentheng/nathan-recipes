Tortellini

A traditional (creamy and healthy) pasta dish that is stuff with mushroom with a wonderful garlic sauce

Ingredients

Tortellini dough/Pasta

  • 300 grams of all-purpose flour
  • 3 eggs

Tortellini filling

  • 1/2 lb cremini mushrooms
  • 1/2 lb maitake or oyster mushrooms
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 shallot
  • 3 sage leaves (chopped finely)
  • 1 cup grated parmigiano

Tortellini Sauce

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 tyeaspoon fresh ground nutmeg
  • 1 cup grated parmigiano

Instructions

Tortellini Dough

  1. Add flour to bowl and make a well in the middle
  2. Add 2 eggs, 3 eggs yolks
  3. Incoporate the flour form the edges into the ggs slowly -> until shaggy dough look
  4. Place dough on flat surface and knead by hand until smooth (5 ish mins)
  5. Wrap dough in plastic wrap (45 mins)
  6. Cut dough into 4 even pieces... take one piece of dough and roll into half inch thckness
  7. Run the dough through pasta roller (widest setting and move until #5 thinness setting)
  8. Repeat with the other pieces of dough
  9. Cut dough in 1-2 inches squares...(they are ready to fill now)
  10. Add pea-sized dot of filling to the center of the square
  11. brush edges with water and close points together to make a taco
  12. Connect the other points. repeats with the rest of the dough

Filling

  1. Finely chop the mushrooms, until 1/4 inch thickness
  2. In a saucepan, add butter, heat up and add garlic, and shallot (all finely chopped)
  3. Cook until translucent, mix well
  4. Add chopped mushroom and cook over medium heat (until no water)
  5. Place the mix into a food processor and make into paste
  6. Adde grated parmigiano and add to salt + pepper to taste, mix well
  7. Put the paste in piping bag

Sauce

  1. Add butter and heavy cream, and heat over medium-high until butter is melted
  2. Stir occasionally to emulsify, to a light simmer and reduce for 3 minutes
  3. Stir in parmigiano and stir until emolsified and melted
  4. Turn off heat and add minced garlic and season with salt + pepper

Assembly

  1. Cook the tortellinis for 2 mins and remove with strainer
  2. Add them to sauce
  3. Add couple of pasta water and toss the tortellinis until fully coated
  4. Serve with grated Parmigiano on top
