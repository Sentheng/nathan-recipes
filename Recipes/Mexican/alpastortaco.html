Al Pastor Tacos

A classic type of tacos meat, al pastor, marinade and pineapple relish

Ingredients

Marinade

  • 4 ancho chiles
  • 5 guajilo chiles (seeds)
  • 180 mL white vinegar
  • 240 mL pineapple juice
  • 3.5 tablespoons 65gachiote paste
  • 10cloves of garlic
  • 2 tablespoons of brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 3 tablespoons kosher salt
  • 4.5 pounds of boneless pork shoulder

Pineapple Relish

  • 1 whole pineapple
  • 1 sweet onion
  • 1 jalapeno
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro
  • Juice and zest of 2 limes
  • 10cloves of garlic

Base

  • Store bought corn tortillas
  • lime wedges

Instructions

  1. For marinade, cover all dried chilis with boiling water and sit until easy to tear
  2. Combine 3/4 cup of white vinegar, 1 cup of pineapple juice, achoite past, garlic, brown sugar, gound cumin, and kosher salt in blender
  3. Add soaked chilies and blend until smooth --> This is your marinade
  4. Slice pork should into half-inche slices, pour over marinade and leave over night
  5. Place each slice of marinated pork on metal skewers stacked
  6. On grill, sear the stack on the hot grates for 2-3 minutes on sides until color forms
  7. Place the alpastor meat on the col side of the grill, cover and roast for 1-2 hours
  8. Once cooked, place upright and slice vertically
  9. For pineapple relish, place pineapple quarters, jalapenos, sliced sweet onion on hot grill until nicely charred
  10. Chopped everything into small pieces
  11. Place in a bowl along with cilantro,juice and zest of two limes and season with salt, mixed
  12. Slighly char corn tortillas, add meat and relish
